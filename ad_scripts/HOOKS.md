# UGC Ad Hook Lines

50 proven hooks organized by emotion. Replace [PRODUCT] and [PROBLEM] with your specifics.

**Rule:** The hook has 1-3 seconds to stop the scroll. Say something that creates an open loop the viewer MUST close.

---

## Curiosity Hooks (10)

These make the viewer think "wait, what?" and keep watching.

| # | Hook Line |
|---|-----------|
| 1 | "Okay why is nobody talking about [PRODUCT]?" |
| 2 | "I found something for [PRICE] dollars that replaced my [EXPENSIVE ALTERNATIVE]." |
| 3 | "This is the [PRODUCT] that broke the internet and I finally tried it." |
| 4 | "Wait... does [PRODUCT] actually work? Let me show you." |
| 5 | "I've been hiding this from you guys but I can't anymore." |
| 6 | "The thing about [PRODUCT] that nobody tells you..." |
| 7 | "I wasn't gonna post this but the results are too good not to share." |
| 8 | "So there's this [PRICE]-dollar [PRODUCT] that does what [EXPENSIVE THING] does." |
| 9 | "Tell me why this [PRODUCT] has forty thousand five-star reviews." |
| 10 | "I need someone to explain to me how [PRODUCT] works this well for [PRICE] dollars." |
