## Template 1: "I Was Skeptical" Testimonial

**Duration:** 15s or 30s
**Emotional Arc:** Doubt -> Trial -> Genuine surprise -> Conviction
**Best For:** Products with a "too good to be true" angle

### Structure

| Time | Section | What Happens |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 0-3s | HOOK | Admit skepticism directly to camera |
| 3-8s | PROBLEM | Describe the specific pain point you had |
| 8-12s | TURNAROUND | Show/describe the moment it actually worked |
| 12-15s | CTA | Direct, no-nonsense recommendation |

### 15s Script (Fill-in-the-blank)

```
[0-3s] HOOK:
"Okay I was SO skeptical about [PRODUCT] but I have to be honest with you."

[3-8s] PROBLEM:
"I've been dealing with [PROBLEM] for [TIME PERIOD] and I've tried [1-2 FAILED SOLUTIONS]."

[8-12s] TURNAROUND:
"But after [USAGE PERIOD] with this thing, [SPECIFIC MEASURABLE RESULT]."

[12-15s] CTA:
"It's like [PRICE] dollars. Just try it."
```

### 30s Script (Fill-in-the-blank)

```
[0-3s] HOOK:
"I'm not gonna lie, when my [FRIEND/FAMILY MEMBER] told me about [PRODUCT] I thought it was a total gimmick."

[3-10s] PROBLEM + FAILED ATTEMPTS:
"I've had [PROBLEM] for [TIME PERIOD]. I tried [SOLUTION 1], [SOLUTION 2], even [EXPENSIVE SOLUTION 3]. Nothing worked for more than [SHORT TIME]."

[10-20s] DISCOVERY + PROOF:
"Then I actually tried [PRODUCT]. [DESCRIBE FIRST USE]. After [TIME PERIOD], [SPECIFIC RESULT 1]. [SPECIFIC RESULT 2 — bonus benefit]."

[20-27s] EMOTIONAL PROOF:
"My [PERSON] even noticed. They asked me [COMPLIMENT/QUESTION ABOUT RESULT]."

[27-30s] CTA:
"It was [PRICE] dollars. If you deal with [PROBLEM], just get one."
```

### Example: Jade Roller (15s)

```
[0-3s] "Okay I was SO skeptical about this jade roller but I have to be honest with you."
[3-8s] "I wake up every morning with the puffiest face. I've tried cold water, eye cream, nothing works."
[8-12s] "But after one week of rolling every morning, the puffiness goes down in literally sixty seconds."
[12-15s] "It's like twenty bucks. Just try it."
```

## Template 2: "Before/After Reveal"

**Duration:** 15s
**Emotional Arc:** Frustration -> Transformation -> Awe
**Best For:** Products with visible results (beauty, cleaning, home, fitness)

### Structure

| Time | Section | What Happens |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 0-3s | HOOK | Show or describe the "before" state |
| 3-8s | THE PRODUCT | Brief intro of what you used |
| 8-12s | THE REVEAL | Show the "after" — let the visual do the work |
| 12-15s | CTA | Price + where to get it |

### Script

```
[0-3s] HOOK (show the problem):
"This is what my [THING] looked like before." / "Look at this [MESS/PROBLEM]."

[3-8s] PRODUCT INTRO:
"I used [PRODUCT] for [TIME/AMOUNT OF EFFORT]. That's it."

[8-12s] REVEAL (show the result):
"And now look at it." [PAUSE — let the visual land] "[REACTION — wow, insane, I can't believe it]."

[12-15s] CTA:
"[PRICE] dollars. Link in bio."
```

### Example: Knife Sharpener (15s)

```
[0-3s] "This is my kitchen knife. It can't even cut through a tomato right now. Embarrassing."
[3-8s] "Three pulls through this sharpener. Took me ten seconds."
[8-12s] "And now look at that." [Show knife slicing paper-thin tomato slices] "Are you kidding me."
[12-15s] "Twenty-two bucks. Link in bio."
```

## Template 3: "Friend Recommendation"

**Duration:** 20s
**Emotional Arc:** Casual warmth -> Relatability -> Trust -> Gentle push
**Best For:** Any product — this format mimics organic word-of-mouth

### Structure

| Time | Section | What Happens |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 0-3s | HOOK | Start mid-conversation, like you just remembered something |
| 3-10s | THE STORY | How you discovered it (through a friend, online, by accident) |
| 10-17s | YOUR EXPERIENCE | What happened when you tried it |
| 17-20s | CTA | Casual, low-pressure recommendation |

### Script

```
[0-3s] HOOK:
"Oh my god okay so my [FRIEND/SISTER/COWORKER] just put me on to [PRODUCT] and I need to tell you about it."

[3-10s] DISCOVERY STORY:
"So [he/she] has been using it for [TIME] because [he/she] [HAD SAME PROBLEM]. And [he/she] was like you HAVE to try this."

[10-17s] YOUR RESULT:
"So I got one and [DESCRIBE EXPERIENCE]. [SPECIFIC RESULT]. I literally [REACTION — texted them back to say thank you / couldn't believe it]."

[17-20s] CTA:
"I'll put the link [below / in my bio]. It's like [PRICE] bucks, you won't regret it."
```

### Example: Blue Light Glasses (20s)

```
[0-3s] "Okay so my coworker just put me on to these blue light glasses and I need to tell you about them."
[3-10s] "She works from home like me and was getting these horrible headaches every day by like three PM. She started wearing these and said the headaches just stopped."
[10-17s] "So I got a pair and after like five days, my eyes stopped being dry and irritated by the end of the day. I literally texted her like why didn't you tell me sooner."
[17-20s] "I'll put the link below. They're like fifteen bucks, you won't regret it."
```

## Template 4: "Angry Rant About the Problem" -> Solution

**Duration:** 30s
**Emotional Arc:** Frustration/anger -> Venting -> Discovery -> Relief -> Gratitude
**Best For:** Products that solve an annoying, universal problem

### Structure

| Time | Section | What Happens |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 0-5s | HOOK (the rant) | Start heated. Raw frustration about the problem. |
| 5-12s | ESCALATION | Pile on failed solutions, wasted money, wasted time |
| 12-20s | THE TURN | Introduce the product. Tone shifts from angry to cautiously hopeful. |
| 20-27s | THE RESULT | Specific proof it worked. Disbelief. Relief. |
| 27-30s | CTA | Direct recommendation with price |

### Script

```
[0-5s] RANT HOOK:
"Can we talk about [PROBLEM] for a second? Because I am OVER IT."

[5-12s] ESCALATION:
"I have spent [AMOUNT] on [FAILED SOLUTION 1]. I've tried [FAILED SOLUTION 2]. I even [EXTREME MEASURE]. And NOTHING works for more than [SHORT TIME]."

[12-20s] THE TURN:
"So last month someone on [Reddit / TikTok / at work] mentioned [PRODUCT]. I bought it for [PRICE] thinking yeah right, another waste of money. But I tried it and [DESCRIBE FIRST USE EXPERIENCE]."

[20-27s] THE RESULT:
"It's been [TIME PERIOD] and [SPECIFIC RESULT]. Like [CONCRETE PROOF]. I genuinely cannot believe a [PRICE]-dollar [PRODUCT] fixed what [EXPENSIVE SOLUTIONS] couldn't."

[27-30s] CTA:
"If you deal with [PROBLEM], please just try this. [PRICE] dollars. I wish I found it sooner."
```

### Example: Posture Corrector (30s)

```
[0-5s] "Can we talk about back pain for a second? Because I am OVER IT."
[5-12s] "I have spent hundreds on a chiropractor. I've tried stretching every morning. I even got a standing desk. And nothing works for more than a day."
[12-20s] "So last month my physical therapist mentioned a posture corrector. I bought this one for twenty-five bucks thinking yeah right, another waste of money. But I wore it under my shirt at work for a few hours a day."
[20-27s] "It's been three weeks and the knot between my shoulder blades is basically gone. Like I forget about my back now because it just doesn't hurt. I cannot believe a twenty-five-dollar brace fixed what hundreds in chiro couldn't."
[27-30s] "If your back is always killing you, please just try this. Twenty-five dollars. I wish I found it sooner."
```

## Template 5: "Morning Routine" Product Placement

**Duration:** 20s
**Emotional Arc:** Relatable everyday -> Natural discovery -> Genuine endorsement
**Best For:** Beauty, wellness, health, kitchen products

### Structure

| Time | Section | What Happens |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 0-3s | HOOK | "My morning routine changed when..." or "Step [X] of my morning" |
| 3-12s | THE ROUTINE | Show the product in natural use. Not a hard sell — it's just part of your life now. |
| 12-17s | THE PAYOFF | Show or describe the result that keeps you coming back |
| 17-20s | CTA | Soft sell — "I'll link it" or "it's only [PRICE]" |

### Script

```
[0-3s] HOOK:
"Step [NUMBER] of my morning routine and honestly the one that makes the biggest difference."

[3-12s] THE ROUTINE (show product in use):
"Every morning I [ACTION WITH PRODUCT]. It takes [TIME] and [DESCRIBE WHAT IT DOES — how it feels, what happens]. I've been doing this for [TIME PERIOD] now."

[12-17s] THE PAYOFF:
"And the difference is [SPECIFIC RESULT]. [SECONDARY BENEFIT]. People keep asking me [COMPLIMENT]."

[17-20s] CTA:
"I'll link it. It's like [PRICE] dollars and it lasts [DURATION]."
```

### Example: Jade Roller (20s)

```
[0-3s] "Step three of my morning routine and honestly the one that makes the biggest difference."
[3-12s] "Every morning after I wash my face I roll with this jade roller for about sixty seconds. Up along the jawline, under the eyes, across the forehead. I've been doing this for a month now."
[12-17s] "And the depuffing is immediate. Like I look awake. My skin has this glow. People keep asking me what I changed."
[17-20s] "I'll link it. It's like twenty bucks and it lasts forever."
```

## Template 6: "Duet/Reaction" Style

**Duration:** 15s
**Emotional Arc:** Curiosity/disbelief -> Watching -> Genuine surprise -> Agreement
**Best For:** Products with a visual "wow" moment or viral clip potential

### Structure

| Time | Section | What Happens |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 0-3s | HOOK | React to a claim or question about the product |
| 3-10s | THE DEMO | Show yourself using/testing the product |
| 10-13s | THE REACTION | Genuine surprise at the result |
| 13-15s | CTA | Confirm it works, drop the price |

### Script

```
[0-3s] HOOK (reacting to an unseen comment/video):
"Wait, people are saying [PRODUCT] actually [CLAIM]? Let me try this." /
"Someone said this [PRICE]-dollar [PRODUCT] is better than [EXPENSIVE ALTERNATIVE]. No way."

[3-10s] THE TEST:
[Show yourself using the product. Visible skepticism on face.] "[ACTION DESCRIPTION]... okay..."

[10-13s] THE REACTION:
"Wait. [PAUSE] That actually works." / "Okay hold on... [LOOK OF GENUINE SURPRISE]... that's insane."

[13-15s] CTA:
"[PRICE] dollars, people. [PRICE] dollars."
```

### Example: Self-Cleaning Hairbrush (15s)

```
[0-3s] "Someone said this hairbrush cleans itself with one button press. No way. Let me try this."
[3-10s] [Show the brush full of hair. Press the retract button.] "Okay pressing it now..."
[10-13s] "Wait." [Hair falls away, brush is clean.] "That's actually insane."
[13-15s] "Twenty dollars, people. Twenty dollars."
```

## Template 8: "Storytime" Narrative

**Duration:** 30s
**Emotional Arc:** Relatable setup -> Rising tension -> Discovery -> Resolution -> Recommendation
**Best For:** Products that solved a real frustration — the longer format lets you build emotional investment

### Structure

| Time | Section | What Happens |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 0-5s | HOOK (storytime start) | "So this is embarrassing but..." or "Story time..." |
| 5-12s | SETUP | The situation. Paint the picture. Make it relatable. |
| 12-18s | THE LOW POINT | It got worse. The moment you knew you had to fix it. |
| 18-25s | THE DISCOVERY | How you found the product. First use. The shift. |
| 25-30s | RESOLUTION + CTA | Where you are now. Price. Recommendation. |

### Script

```
[0-5s] HOOK:
"Story time. So [EMBARRASSING/RELATABLE SITUATION SETUP]."

[5-12s] SETUP:
"For [TIME PERIOD] I was dealing with [PROBLEM]. Like [VIVID SPECIFIC DETAIL]. It was getting to the point where [CONSEQUENCE — social, physical, financial]."

[12-18s] LOW POINT:
"The worst part was [SPECIFIC MOMENT]. That's when I was like okay I need to fix this."

[18-25s] DISCOVERY:
"I found [PRODUCT] on [WHERE]. It was only [PRICE] so I figured worth a shot. [DESCRIBE FIRST USE]. And [RESULT — immediate or after X days]."

[25-30s] RESOLUTION + CTA:
"It's been [TIME] now and [CURRENT STATE]. If you deal with [PROBLEM], this is the one thing that actually worked for me. I'll link it."
```

### Example: Pet Grooming Glove (30s)

```
[0-5s] "Story time. So I love my dog more than anything but I almost lost my mind over the shedding."
[5-12s] "For like six months every surface in my apartment was covered in golden retriever fur. My couch, my clothes, my car. I found fur in my coffee once."
[12-18s] "The worst part was having people over and being embarrassed. I was vacuuming twice a day and it didn't matter. That's when I was like okay I need to fix this."
[18-25s] "Someone at the dog park told me about these grooming gloves. They were twelve bucks so I figured worth a shot. My dog thought she was just getting pet. And the amount of fur that came off in one session was disgusting. In a good way."
[25-30s] "It's been two months and my apartment is actually clean. If your pet sheds, this is the one thing that actually worked. I'll link it."
```

## Template 9: "POV" First-Person

**Duration:** 15s
**Emotional Arc:** Immersion -> Recognition -> Payoff
**Best For:** Products with a strong visual moment or relatable daily situation

### Structure

| Time | Section | What Happens |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 0-2s | TEXT OVERLAY HOOK | "POV: [RELATABLE SITUATION]" — text on screen |
| 2-10s | THE SCENE | Show the experience from first person. No talking needed — the visual IS the content. |
| 10-13s | THE PAYOFF | The product delivers. Show the result. |
| 13-15s | CTA | Text overlay or quick voiceover with product name + price |

### Script

```
[0-2s] TEXT OVERLAY:
"POV: [RELATABLE SITUATION WITH PRODUCT]"

[2-10s] THE SCENE (visual storytelling, minimal or no talking):
[Show hands using product / show the environment / show the process]

[10-13s] THE PAYOFF:
[Show the satisfying result — the reveal, the reaction, the clean surface, the transformation]

[13-15s] CTA (text overlay + optional voiceover):
"[PRODUCT NAME] — [PRICE] — link in bio"
```

### Example: Galaxy Star Projector (15s)

```
[0-2s] TEXT: "POV: You spent thirty dollars and turned your bedroom into outer space"
[2-10s] [Show hand plugging in projector. Room lights turn off. Stars and nebula slowly fill the ceiling and walls. Camera pans slowly across the galaxy effect.]
[10-13s] [Camera settles on the full room transformation. Hold the shot. Let it breathe.]
[13-15s] TEXT: "Galaxy Star Projector — $30 — link in bio"
```

## Template 10: "Whisper/ASMR Review"

**Duration:** 20s
**Emotional Arc:** Intimacy -> Sensory detail -> Trust -> Soft close
**Best For:** Beauty, self-care, sensory products, anything with satisfying textures/sounds

### Structure

| Time | Section | What Happens |
|------|---------|-------------|
| 0-3s | HOOK | Whispered or soft-spoken hook. Close to mic. Intimate. |
| 3-10s | SENSORY DETAIL | Describe how the product feels, sounds, looks. SHOW the texture. |
| 10-17s | THE EXPERIENCE | Describe what it does for you. Keep voice soft and genuine. |
| 17-20s | CTA | Whispered recommendation. Ultra-soft close. |

### Script

```
[0-3s] HOOK (whispered, close to camera):
"Okay if you haven't tried [PRODUCT] yet... you're missing out."

[3-10s] SENSORY DETAIL (soft voice, show product up close):
"Feel this. [DESCRIBE TEXTURE/WEIGHT/TEMPERATURE]. And when you [USE IT], it [SENSORY DESCRIPTION — the sound, the feeling, the visual]."

[10-17s] THE EXPERIENCE:
"I use it [WHEN/HOW OFTEN] and [RESULT]. [SECONDARY SENSORY BENEFIT]. It's become my favorite part of my [ROUTINE]."

[17-20s] CTA (whispered):
"It's [PRICE] dollars. Link in bio. You'll thank me."
```

### Example: Heatless Curling Set (20s)

```
[0-3s] [Whispered] "Okay if you haven't tried heatless curls yet... you're missing out."
[3-10s] [Show the soft satin rod up close] "It's this soft satin rod. You wrap your damp hair around it before bed. It doesn't pull, it doesn't hurt, you barely feel it."
[10-17s] "I wrap it up in five minutes, sleep on it, and in the morning..." [Show the curl reveal — bouncy, voluminous curls] "...these. No heat. No damage. My hair actually feels softer."
[17-20s] [Whispered] "It's twenty-two dollars. Link in bio. You'll thank me."
```

## Variation Matrix

One script becomes 12 ads:

| Variable | Options |
|----------|---------|
| Hook style | Direct / Question / Shocking statement |
| Pacing | Fast (TikTok energy) / Slow (trust-building) |
| CTA | Soft ("I'll link it") / Hard ("Buy this now") |

**3 hooks x 2 pacing x 2 CTAs = 12 unique variations from 1 script.**
